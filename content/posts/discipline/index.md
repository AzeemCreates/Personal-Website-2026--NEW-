Started my day at 3:30 AM reflecting on what discipline actually means. Every human is addicted to something. Discipline can be the true way of life.

Find balance, but do not be content with what you have just yet. Everything rough in your life — take it as a lesson and learn. Build something. Serve your community.

I am running on 20 hours of sleep this week and still pushing through. There is no excuse. The real gauge is how bad you actually want it.

Discipline protects peace.
