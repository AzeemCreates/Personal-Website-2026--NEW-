Very blessed to have the opportunity to attend the Microsoft event in Washington, DC. It was my first time in DC traveling with my Director and I would definitely do it again.

Got to see a thought provoking speech by the President/VP for Microsoft, Chris Barry — he talked about the future of AI and innovation. Meeting veterans and those who dedicated their lives to public service was an awakening experience.

Also meeting our IBM colleagues Hemang Shah, John Forde, and Ben Lyons — having genuine conversations about my AI project and ways to make systems more efficient.

All in all, great experience with genuine people and conversations. To 2026.
